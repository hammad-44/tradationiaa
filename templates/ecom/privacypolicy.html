Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Stitch & Bride ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and handles your information when you use our online stitching service website and explore our exquisite bridal wear products.

Information Collected

We collect various types of information, including personal details like your name, contact information, and measurements when you engage with our stitching service. Additionally, we gather non-personal information such as browsing history, IP addresses, and cookies to enhance your website experience and provide valuable insights for our analytics.

Use of Information

Your information is used to tailor our services to your preferences, process orders seamlessly, and communicate with you effectively. With your consent, we may also use the collected data for personalized marketing to keep you informed about our latest bridal wear collections and promotions.

Data Sharing

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate our operations and improve our services. Rest assured, we only collaborate with partners who adhere to stringent data protection standards.

Security Measures

Ensuring the security of your information is our priority. We employ industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. In the unfortunate event of a data breach, we will promptly notify you and take appropriate action to mitigate any potential risks.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or would like to exercise your privacy rights, please contact our privacy team at privacy@stitchandbride.com. We appreciate your trust in Stitch & Bride.
