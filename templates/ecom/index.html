HOW IT WORKS

Effortlessly transform your ideas into reality with our streamlined stitching services website process, blending precision and creativity for a perfect fit.

PLACE YOUR ORDER

Place your order online to book a stitch at your home or you can also place an order by visiting our workshop.

PICKING YOUR SAMPLE DRESS

The rider will come to pick your dress material along with the sample dress that fits you perfectly or hand over it on our workshop.

STITCH IN PROGRESS

We will stitch the design of your choices or you can select designs from our product design catalog.

DELIVERY IN 7 DAYS

Your custom-tailored dress along with your sample dress will be properly packaged and it will be safely and securely delivered at your doorstep within 7-10 days
