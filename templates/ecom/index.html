HOW IT WORKS

Effortlessly transform your ideas into reality with our streamlined stitching services website process, blending precision and creativity for a perfect fit.

PLACE YOUR ORDER

International customers are advised to place orders on WhatsApp (+92 3154419916) . The shipping charges will depend upon weight. Once you've confirmed the order, our customer support representative will let you know the shipping charges.

STITCH IN PROGRESS

Our experienced and talented tailors will stitch the dresses according to the size provided by customers. The size and design will be discussed over WhatsApp.

Shipment Method

Once we receive the payment, the order is properly checked and then dispatched through an international courier company, i.e., DHL or Skynet. Standard delivery time is 8-10 days.

Payment Method

We receive payment directly in our bank account or through Western Union. Details of which will be shared with you once the order is confirmed.
